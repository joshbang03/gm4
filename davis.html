My Opinion of the Best Spots In Davis

Burgers and Brew

Located downtown, Burgers and Brew offers some of the best gourmet burgers in Davis. Their garlic fries are a must-try!

Open Rice Kitchen

Open Rice Kitchen is a student favorite, known for its affordable price and tasty selection. Try their pork belly fried rice!

Pho Sit Lo

Go to spot in Davis for soup. Their pho broth is very light and give a lot of beef!
